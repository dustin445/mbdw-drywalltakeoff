Export Buttons

Both buttons call the same exportToCSV function. The difference is whether pricing data is included in the output.
EXPORT W/OUT PRICING

<JobName>_order_for_pricing.csv
Intended to be sent to purchasing / supplier to fill in prices, then imported back into the app.
  • Pricing Sheet — one row per material with a blank $/SQ FT << FILL IN column FILL IN
  • Accessory Pricing — one row per accessory with a blank Unit Price << FILL IN column FILL IN
  • Order Details — board counts per area, sheet totals, sq ft NO PRICES
  • Accessories List — product, qty, placement NO PRICES
MPR EXPORT

<JobName>_MPR.csv
An internal pricing report (MPR = Material Purchase Report). Includes everything in the other export, plus full costing.
  • Per-material $/sq ft and total cost for every area WITH PRICES
  • Accessories with unit prices and line totals WITH PRICES
  • Cartage calculation
  • Total Material Cost (boards + accessories + cartage)
  • Full Labour Budget section: boarding, taping, beading, scrap, drives, management, warranty, scaffold
  • Overhead % and Profit % applied on top of material + labour
  • Site notes (if any)
On mobile: both buttons use the Web Share API (native share sheet on Android/iOS).
On desktop: falls back to a standard file download.
